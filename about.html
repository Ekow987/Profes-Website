Our History

Track Record of Excellence and Value Creation

Prefos Limited was incorporated on the 9th of January 1991 with only ten employees. Through hard work and resilience, Prefos Limited has become one of the largest and the most sophisticated electrical engineering companies in Ghana, employing over 150 permanent and non-permanent staff throughout Ghana. Spurred on by the vision to become the most innovative and competitive electrical engineering company in Ghana and the West African sub-region, Prefos has played a significant role in Ghana’s rural/urban electrification drive for 28 years. We have partnered with the government of Ghana through the Public Private Partnership (PPP) initiative to deliver on key projects in various Metropolitan, Municipal and District Assemblies (MMDAs) making Prefos Ltd a household name associated with quality service. Our company is dedicated to lighting up the streets of Ghana to keep them safe and beautiful. We also promote commercial activity by supporting the government of Ghana to create jobs through the installation of street lighting technology and applications. Our capacity for quality work and timely project delivery has earned our company some high profile clientele from the public and private sectors nationally and internationally over the years. They include the Government of Ghana (past and present), various sector ministries, public and private institutions, among others. Prefos is working to extend its services to other countries in the West African sub-region as part of our strategic expansion initiative.

STRATEGIC INVESTMENT

Ultra-Modern Factory

Prefos inaugurated its ultra-modern factory complex in Adako-Jachie, near Kumasi in 2014. The inauguration was attended by several dignitaries including Otumfuo Osei Tutu 11, the King of the Asante Kingdom and a retinue of chiefs, President J.A.K Kufour, former President of the Republic of Ghana, Ministers of State, Ambassadors and Members of the Diplomatic corps, partners and leading members of the Ghana National Chamber of Commerce and Industry (GNCCI) the Association of Ghana Industries (AGI), among others.Our quest to become a major supplier of street lighting technology in Ghana and the West African market culminated in the construction of an ultra-modern factory with state-of-the-art facilities at Adako-Jachie near Ejisu in the Ashanti Region for assembling imported street light components for distribution. Facilities in the factory include a modern street lamp assembling line, a testing laboratory and a show room.Again, as part of efforts to train and equip the youth to carry out post-installation maintenance and servicing of street lights in their communities, the company has established the Prefos Electrical Training College (PETC) in Kumasi. The facility, which has a hostel accommodation of 50 rooms, will provide residential facilitates for trainees. It is expected to create over 2500 jobs for youths from the various Metropolitan, Municipal and District Assemblies (MMDAs) within the next few years, thus helping to address unemployment problems particularly among the rural youth.
