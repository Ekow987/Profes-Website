Leadership in Street Lighting Technology

There is no doubt that Prefos is one of the biggest electrical engineering companies in Ghana today. After 31 years of continuous service to God and country, Prefos has become synonymous with providing modern and efficient street lighting solutions that extend to the remotest corners of Ghana. “As with every profession, Prefos has had its share of challenges, some of them quite intimidating, but God has always been our strength and we will continue to look up to Him for wisdom to ride the storm,” he said. Mr. Fosu’s passion to support youth training and development has culminated in a job creation and philanthropy program that has benefitted all sectors of society. Prefos is in the process of establishing another assembling plant to introduce modern sophisticated LED technology to feed the West African market. The company is also poised to commence manufacturing of electrical cables and accessories in Ghana soon. “Our prospects are bright because we provide quality service to our clients, and we seek to introduce the latest technology into our operations,”.

MR. ALEXIE K.FOSU
