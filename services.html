Lighting for Security and Beautification

Prefos Limited is most celebrated for its flagship street lighting and beautification projects. For many years, we have provided reliable, durable and latest technologies in street lighting for the Ghanaian and West African markets. Our objective in lighting up the streets and roads of Ghana and other West African countries is to enhance security and protect life and property in our rapidly growing urban settlements. We also seek to enhance the beauty of our environment, promote commercial activities and facilitate harmonious individual and group activities. Thanks to our assembling plant at Adako Jachie in the Ashanti Region, we are able to supply lighting equipment and components for maintenance and repair works on our streets. We also manufacture LED street lights which are fast replacing the conventional lamp systems due to their overall efficiency. They are also preferred by many clients due to their ability to achieve lighting uniformity.
